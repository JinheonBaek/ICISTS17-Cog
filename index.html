The Computer Recognizes Your Various Emotions!

Game Guide

  • 1. Roll the dice.
  • 2. See the table below, and check emotion that is matched to your dice number.
  • 3. Express your emotion in front of this computer camera.
  • 4. Press the Take Snapshot Button as maintaing your face emotion.
  • 5. Before press Analysing Image button, confirm your name and image.
  • 6. Press the Analysing Image button, and wait a moment for handling your image.
  • 7. If your score is higher than threshold, you will get some gifts!
    •
Dice Number Emotion Threshold Difficulty Description
